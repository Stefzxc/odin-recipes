Ice soup

Description

The perfect meal tailored to when the paycheck hasn't come in yet and you spent the entire last one on repairs for your wall because LARRY BLEW A HOLE IN IT WITH HIS STUPID ROOMBA AND MADE YOU PAY FOR THE REPAIRS

Ingredients

  • water
  • ice cubes
  • (optional) ice sphere

Steps

  1. chill your water in a device i am informed is commonly called a "fridge"
  2. pour your water into a bowl and grate in 1 large ice cube before stirring
  3. finish with a scattering of small ice cubes and if possible a single large ice sphere
